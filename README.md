# Popcorn Vision

[![Popcorn Vision](/src/assets/popcorn-vision.jpg)](https://www.popcorn.vision)

[![Website](https://img.shields.io/badge/Website-Visit%20Now-blue)](https://www.popcorn.vision)

is your ultimate source for movie and TV show information, now available as a Progressive Web App (PWA)! Say goodbye to the hassle of constantly switching to your browser. With Popcorn Vision, you can have instant access to all the details you need, right on your device.

## Features

- **Extensive Database:** Explore a vast collection of movies and TV series, from timeless classics to the latest releases.
- **Detailed Information:** Dive deep into your favorite titles with comprehensive synopses, reviews, ratings, and cast information.
- **Convenient Search Functionality:** Find exactly what you're looking for with our user-friendly search feature.
- **Install as PWA:** Install Popcorn Vision as a PWA on your device for seamless browsing and instant access.

## Get Started

Visit our [website](https://www.popcorn.vision) to discover the world of movies and TV shows. You can also enhance your experience by installing Popcorn Vision as an app:

- Open the website in your browser.
- Look for the install prompt or the option to "Add to Home Screen."
- Follow the on-screen instructions to install Popcorn Vision as a PWA on your device.

Don't miss out on the convenience of having movie and TV show information at your fingertips!
